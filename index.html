Welcome to Collaborative Hackathon Fall 2025

The Collaborative Hackathon Fall 2025 challenges students from Virginia State University and National Taipei University to design, prototype, and present mobile applications (iOS or Android) that solve real-world problems through innovation, usability, and strong visual design. The hackathon encourages creativity, cross-border teamwork, and a focus on user-centered development.

Objectives of the Hackathon

  • Promote international collaboration between VSU and NTPU students.
  • Foster creativity in mobile app design and prototype development.
  • Encourage structured design thinking and strong UI/UX practices.
  • Provide experience in presenting and pitching ideas effectively.
