Welcome to Collaborative Hackathon Fall 2025

The Collaborative Hackathon Fall 2025 challenges students from Virginia State University and National Taipei University to design, prototype, and present mobile applications that solve real-world problems through innovation, usability, and strong visual design.

Objectives

  • Promote international collaboration between VSU and NTPU students.
  • Encourage creativity in application design and prototyping.
  • Build strong UI/UX and structured design thinking skills.
  • Develop presentation and pitching experience.

Why Participate?

Gain hands-on design experience, collaborate internationally, enhance your resume, and compete for exciting prizes. This is your opportunity to turn your ideas into compelling app prototypes.

Prizes

Top teams will receive certificates, gadgets, and cash prizes celebrating creativity and innovation.
